What is Arky? To recognize contributions

In our current society, we slogged all our life trying to be somebody on this planet. We aim high, work hard and often wish that people recognize us for all the hard work we had done. However, for majority of us, we are still nobody in the end. Our limited social circle has crippled and denied us the honor we rightfully deserve.

To provide opportunities and space to honor people’s contribution in our planet and encouraging all human to be more appreciative of others’ contribution.

So how it works? It's easy.

Individuals will be choosen randomly daily and be highlighted as the Captain of the day. His/her information, together with 4 other individuals, will be featured on the frontpage!

And lastly, invite everyone! Spread the words.

Let everybody's contribution to the society to be recognized! Get your friends and family to register today!
